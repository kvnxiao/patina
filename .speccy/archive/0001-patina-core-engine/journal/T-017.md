  it. `timestamp_to_rfc3339` reformats the compact `YYYYMMDDTHHMMSSZ`
  journal `<ts>` into the RFC 3339 `at` field.

  path (`build_apply_record`): each completed `Symlink` records its
  canonical link target; each `Copy`/`Render` reads the just-written bytes

  what `status` recomputes. `last_apply.user`/`host` come from the
  resolver's `patina.user`/`patina.hostname` built-ins.
